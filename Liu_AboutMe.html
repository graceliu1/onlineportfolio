About Me

I am a freshman student here at Dublin High School taking my first engineering based course!


Hello! My name is Grace Liu and I am currently a freshman here at Dublin High School. I am interested in software engineering and hope to develop a solid background to reach my goal of working in a field of computer science.

To accomplish this, I am participating in several STEM-related activities allowing me to gain more experience. For example, I am a member of a Gael Force Robotics competition team, and participating in hackathons with a team of students. I have also founded a club called Girls Math Association at my high school. Currently, I am a member of the software development path of the Dublin High Engineering Academy (DEDA). I have previous experience in Java and Python. By the end of my high school career, I hope to have sufficiently developed a basis for any job I am considering in the future.

So why did I choose to pursue a career in STEM? In short, it was because of its relevance and opportunities for future development. Technology has become such a central aspect of our lives, and the world needs more engineers. I aspire to make an impact in any field I will work in, and I believe I can do so in engineering.

Even though I remain focused on these pursuits, I still continue my participation in other extracurricular activities. As of now, I have been playing piano for eight years. After freshman year, I plan to continue playing piano, regardless of as an competitive activity or hobby. Music has now become a way for me to relax. I am part of a music organization called Music For You consisting of students across the Pleasanton, Dublin, and San Ramon. We regularly perform at senior homes near us. In my free time, I enjoy drawing, playing badminton with my friends, poetry, and singing. Overall, my participation in team events has helped me learn how to effectively work with others towards a common goal. From my different experiences, I have learned that I value creativity and determination. I am extremely adaptable and open towards learning new concepts. When working with others, I often take the role of leader, but I also a flexible follower.

As a student, I strive to maintain a balance between my academics and extracurriculars. I hope that you have learned more about me and my values from reading this page. To contact me, please go to my Contact Me page. Feel free to explore the rest of this website!
