About Me

Hello, my name is Grace Liu and I am a senior at Dublin High School. I'm interested in software engineering and hope to achieve my goal of working in artificial intelligence and machine learning after college.

So far, I have taken Honors Principles of Engineering (2019-2020), AP Computer Science Principles (2018-2019), and AP Computer Science Applications (2020-2021). These classes have helped me gain basic knowledge about different programming languages. This school year, I am taking AP Computer Science Applications. As a member of the software development path of the Dublin High Engineering and Design Academy, I strive to develop my programming skills. This year, I have learned basic Java. I am currently working on learning algorithms.

In addition to school courses, I am participating in several science and engineering related activities in order to gain more experience. This year, I am the co-president of the Girls Who Code club at Dublin High School. I have also been a member of the Gael Force Robotics competition teams for the past two years. During my freshman year, I founded a club called the Girls Math Association to encourage young women to do mathematics. Likewise, I have participated in prestigious math competitions to improve my problem-solving skills. I have previous experience in Java and Python.

Aside from engineering, I enjoy reading, playing piano, drawing, and playing badminton with my friends. My experiences have taught me the value of creativity and determination. Overall, I am adaptable and open towards learning new concepts. I'm always willing to work on new projects with peers.

So why did I choose to pursue a career in STEM? In short, it was because of the many opportunities for future development. Technology has become a central aspect of our lives in the modern world. To keep up with the demand, more engineers are needed. I aspire to contribute to technological progress. I hope that you have learned more about me and my values from reading this page. To contact me, please go to my Contact Me page. Feel free to explore the rest of this website!
