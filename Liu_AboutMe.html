About Me

I am a freshman student here at Dublin High School taking my first engineering based course!


Hello, my name is Grace Liu and I am a sophomore at Dublin High School. I am interested in software engineering and hope to achieve my goal of working in artificial intelligence and machine learning after college. By the end of my high school career, I hope to have sufficiently developed a basis for higher level education in the future.

Last year, I took Computer Science Principles (CSP) in the 2018-2019 school year to gain basic knowledge about different programming languages. Currently, I am taking Honors Principles of Engineering. As a member of the software development path of the Dublin High Engineering and Design Academy, I strive to develop my coding skills. Next year, I plan to take AP Computer Science Applications as one of my electives.

In addition to school courses, I am participating in several science and engineering related activities in order to gain more experience. For example, I am a member of 5327C, a Gael Force Robotics competition team. This year, I am also the secretary of the Girls Who Code club at Dublin High School. In the 2018-2019 school year, I founded a club called Girls Math Association. I have previous experience in Java and Python. Aside from engineering, I enjoy reading, playing piano, drawing, and playing badminton with my friends. My experiences have taught me the value of creativity and determination. Overall, I am adaptable and open towards learning new concepts. When working with others, I often take the role of leader, but I am also a flexible follower.

So why did I choose to pursue a career in STEM? In short, it was because of the many opportunities for future development. Technology has become a central aspect of our lives in the modern world. To keep up with the demand, more engineers are needed. I aspire to contribute to technological progress. I hope that you have learned more about me and my values from reading this page.
